my education

marine academy of technology & environmental science

my favorite classes were introduction to computer science and astronomy/geology.

a good memory was an event my school used to hold called "coffee house," student bands would perform in the cafeteria room and there'd be video game tournaments and game rooms too. my friends and i would get food, listen to the music, and then go upstairs to the game rooms and play video games together.

stevens institute of technology

my favorite classes so far have been cs-306: introduction to IT security, and har 180: art history, prehistoric to modern.

one of my favorite memories of my college career was last year when i went to the metropolitan museum of art with my roommate! we got to see so much of the museum and it was so beautiful. i especially loved the hallway for impressionism.
